In an insightful Press Reader socialization held on November 5, 2024, at the Adam Kurniawan Library, Mr. Bayu Setiawan as a Business Development Executive and Ms. Natasa Tjahjana as the President Director from PT. Solusi Edukasi International digital media specialist explained the key features of PressReader, a platform offering access to thousands of newspapers and magazines worldwide, and demonstrated how the platform vast digital library allows users to read publications in their original formats, offering a truly immersive reading experience. The seminar explored PressReader advantages for readers and learned how PressReader user- friendly interface and vast selection of content are transforming the way people stay informed, offering greater convenience and flexibility in accessing news. Overall, the seminar was running smoothly, offering both an informative and engaging platform for attendees to learn about the digital revolution in news and magazine consumption and how we access information.
