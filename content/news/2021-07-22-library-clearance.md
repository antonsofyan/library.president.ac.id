We hereby inform you that starting **July 23, 2021**, for students who have completed their studies and need a Library Clearance Letter but have not submitted their CD to the library, they can send a thesis soft file in PDF format to email: [library.cdthesis@president.ac.id](mailto:library.cdthesis@president.ac.id) with file name format: **Thesis\_Fullname\_StudentID** by fulfilling the requirements below:

1.  The soft file must include:
    *   a. Approval Sheet (fully signed)
    *   b. Originality Sheet (signed), _see attachment_
    *   c. Recommendation Sheet (if any, signed)
    *   d. Scientific Publication Approval for Academic Interest (signed), _see attachment_
    *   e. Advisor Approval Sheet for publication in the journal/repository/proceeding/unpublish  
        (choose one, signed), _see attachment_
    *   f. The results of the Turnitin similarity check
2.  Return all books and pay library fines if any.

Forms **b, d,** and **e** can be downloaded through: [http://bit.ly/LibraryClearance](http://bit.ly/LibraryClearance)

Thank you.  
**Adam Kurniawan Library**
