On Wednesday, April 22, 2026, the President University Library successfully conducted a Journal Publishing Webinar in collaboration with World Scientific Publishing. The session brought together lecturers and students for an engaging discussion on the academic publishing process, covering key stages such as selecting suitable journals, navigating peer review, and achieving publication.

Venkatesh Sandhya, Assistant Director (Social Sciences) at World Scientific Publishing, delivered the webinar. Drawing on her extensive experience, she provided practical insights into the publishing lifecycle, including how manuscripts are evaluated, what publishers look for in submissions, and how authors can increase the visibility and impact of their work. Her experience of working with a wide range of authors, including leading scholars, offered participants valuable insights.

From the library’s perspective, the webinar emphasised the importance of promoting academic literacy and responsible research practices. In particular, the discussions touched on the growing use of generative AI in academic writing. It was emphasised that the application of AI in publishing can be managed through clear written guidelines provided by journals, as well as through continued collaboration between World Scientific Publishing and the President University Library.

This webinar also marks the beginning of further initiatives between the library and the publisher. Additional workshops and training sessions are being planned to support students and lecturers in developing their research and publication skills, thereby reinforcing the university’s commitment to building a strong and ethical academic culture.
