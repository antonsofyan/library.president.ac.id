Hello Presunivers and Researchers of President University, You can access e-Journals using Springer, especially during online lectures and research activities from home like today. Adam Kurniawan Library will inform you how to use the Springer website. The Springer website will help Presunivers to be able to access scientific references in e-Journals subscribed to by President University. Come on, optimize access to e-Journals, e-books and other electronic references that have been subscribed by President University. Make e-journals and e-books as research references and preparation of Scientific Papers of the President University Academic Community. If there are difficulties or problems, please contact our reference via email reference@president.ac.id
