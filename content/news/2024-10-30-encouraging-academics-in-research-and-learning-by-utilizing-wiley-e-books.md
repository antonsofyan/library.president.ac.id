On October 10, 2024, President University hosted an informative seminar titled “Encouraging Academics in Research and Learning by Utilizing Wiley E-Books.” The event took place in the university auditorium and included two sessions: the first from 11:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. and the second from 1:00 p.m. to 2:00 p.m.

The seminar attracted a diverse audience of students eager to explore the valuable resources offered by Wiley. The keynote speaker, Mr. Nandana, delivered an engaging presentation on effectively using Wiley E-Books, offering practical tips for maximizing their potential in academic research and learning.

During the sessions, participants learned how to navigate the Wiley platform, access a vast array of e-books, and utilize various tools to enhance their academic work. The speaker emphasized the importance of integrating these resources into research practices and how they can support learning across disciplines.

The event encouraged a collaborative atmosphere, with attendees actively participating in discussions and asking questions. Feedback from participants highlighted the practical insights gained and the seminar’s relevance to their academic pursuits.

As the day concluded, the seminar was deemed a resounding success, leaving attendees inspired and equipped with the knowledge to leverage Wiley E-Books in their research and learning journeys. This event further solidified President University’s commitment to promoting academic excellence and innovation.(Windi Ayu Setianingrum/Librarian)
