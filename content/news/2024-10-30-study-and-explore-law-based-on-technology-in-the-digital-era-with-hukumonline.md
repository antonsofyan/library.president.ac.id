On October 15, 2024, the Adam Kurniawan Library hosted an insightful seminar titled “Study and Explore Law Based on Technology in the Digital Era with HukumOnline.” The event took place in the university auditorium and gathered students, faculty, and legal professionals interested in the intersection of law and technology.

The seminar began at 11:00 a.m. with a keynote presentation focused on effectively utilizing HukumOnline, a prominent platform for legal resources and information. The speaker provided attendees with a comprehensive overview of the platform’s features, emphasizing its role in facilitating legal research and education in the digital age.

Participants learned how to navigate HukumOnline to access a wide range of legal materials, including articles, case studies, and regulatory updates. The keynote speaker also shared best practices for incorporating these resources into academic and professional work, highlighting the platform’s potential to enhance understanding of contemporary legal issues.

The interactive format of the seminar encouraged lively discussions, with attendees asking questions and sharing their experiences related to legal technology. The event successfully created an engaging environment for learning and networking among those passionate about law.

As the seminar concluded, participants expressed enthusiasm about the insights gained and the practical skills learned for using HukumOnline effectively. Overall, the event was deemed a great success, reinforcing President University’s commitment to advancing legal education and embracing technological innovation in the field of law. (Windi Ayu Setianingrum/Librarian)
