On Wednesday (12/10), Adam Kurniawan Library held an event titled “Literacy Information: Writing Papers and Ethics in Publishing” for the President University students in the B. I. Corner Adam Kurniawan Library. Dr. Jeanne Francoise, The First Doctor of Defense Heritage in Indonesia came as a speaker at the event given information to the students on how to write papers and also the publishing ethics.

The event was meant for the students to practice and have the experience to write research papers effectively. Dr. Francoise taught the students how to start writing papers, choose a research topic, and how to submit a paper to a journal publisher. After attending the event, the students are expected to know how to improve their writing skills and ethics in publishing. (Anindya/Librarian).
