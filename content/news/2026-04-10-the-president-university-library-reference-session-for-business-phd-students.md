On Saturday, April 4, 2026, Laurensius, a staff member of the President University Library, conducted a class session for doctoral students from the Faculty of Business at Batavia Tower. The session focused on introducing the various academic journals subscribed to by the library, as well as the range of electronic resources (e-resources) available to support research and academic activities.

During the session, participants were guided through key databases and platforms accessible through the library, with an emphasis on how to effectively search, evaluate, and utilize scholarly materials. The presentation also highlighted the importance of leveraging institutional subscriptions to access high-quality and credible academic sources.

The class was well-received by the doctoral students, who actively engaged in discussions and inquiries throughout the session. This initiative reflects the library’s ongoing commitment to enhancing research literacy and supporting the academic success of President University students, particularly at the postgraduate level.
