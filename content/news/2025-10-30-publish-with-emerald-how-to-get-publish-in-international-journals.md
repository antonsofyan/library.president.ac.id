On October 31, 2025, the Adam Kurniawan Library successfully held a webinar via Google Meet. The event brought together lecturers and postgraduate students to gain practical insights into publishing in reputable international journals.

Mr. Irwan Sukardi, Business Manager at Emerald Publishing, was the main speaker. In his presentation, Mr. Sukardi shared key strategies for developing strong research manuscripts, understanding journal scopes, and managing the submission and peer-review process. He also discussed common challenges authors face and offered tips to improve publication success.

The webinar provided an engaging platform for participants to interact directly with an experienced publishing industry professional. During the Q&A session, attendees raised various questions about publication ethics, journal selection, and the importance of research visibility. Mr. Sukardi responded thoughtfully, offering practical advice and encouragement for early-career researchers.

The event concluded with appreciation expressed to Mr. Irwan Sukardi and all participants for their active involvement. Through this session, the academic community gained valuable knowledge and motivation to pursue publication opportunities that contribute to global scholarly dialogue and enhance institutional research visibility.
