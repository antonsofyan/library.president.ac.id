# Understanding and Preventing Childhood  
Infectious Diseases

On Friday (15/09), Adam Kurniawan Library in cooperation with Komite President Special Needs Center held an event titled “Mengenal dan Mencegah Penyakit Menular Pada Anak”. The keynote speaker is Dr. Sita Ariyani Sp.A., an expert in social pediatrics.

The purpose of this information event is to inform and advise parents in relation to their healthy and sick children, to monitor and ensure the healthy development of all children within the reach of their responsibility, as well as to cooperate with all social institutions in case of special problems concerning children. A special focus of these tasks is to take care of chronically ill and handicapped children and to act as an expert in all problems and legal proceedings (Anindya/Librarian).
