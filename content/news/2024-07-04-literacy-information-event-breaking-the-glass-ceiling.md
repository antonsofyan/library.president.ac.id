27th of June 2024, from 1 – 2.30 pm, the Adam Kurniawan Library hosted an inspiring event centered on the theme “Breaking the Glass Ceiling” for women’s careers. The keynote speaker, Dr. Christina Liem, delivered a powerful address to an intimate audience of around 30 attendees, emphasizing the importance of gender equality and empowerment in the professional sphere.

Dr. Liem, a renowned advocate for women’s rights and an accomplished academic, captivated the audience with her insights and personal anecdotes. She highlighted women’s challenges in various industries and shared strategies for overcoming these obstacles.

“Breaking the glass ceiling is not just a metaphor,” Dr. Liem began. “It’s a reality that many women encounter daily. Our goal is to recognize these barriers and actively dismantle them.”

The event featured an interactive Q&A session where attendees engaged with Dr. Liem, discussing topics ranging from mentorship and networking to work-life balance and leadership development. Participants left the session feeling motivated and equipped with practical advice to advance their careers.

The Adam Kurniawan Library’s commitment to hosting such events underscores the importance of fostering an environment where women can thrive and break through professional barriers. As the session concluded, the sense of camaraderie and shared purpose among the attendees was palpable.

Stay tuned for more events and initiatives aimed at empowering women in their careers, as the journey to break the glass ceiling continues. (Windi/Librarian)
