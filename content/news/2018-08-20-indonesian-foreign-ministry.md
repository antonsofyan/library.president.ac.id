Adam Kurniawan Library, President University – On August 19, 1945, the Indonesian Foreign Ministry was formed. The 73rd anniversary of Indonesia Foreign Ministry. The vision is “The realization of diplomatic authority to strengthen national identity as Maritime State for the Interest of the People.”

With the establishment of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the aims and objectives to be achieved include formulating, establishing and implementing policies in the fields of politics and foreign relations.

In addition, the management of state assets is the responsibility of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, then supervision of the implementation of duties within the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and finally the purpose of the formation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs is the implementation of technical activities from the center to the regions.
