On December 10, 2024, Technical Guidance (Bimtek) Towards Universities Achieving Library Accreditation was held at President University, Cikarang, starting at 09.00 to 15.00 WIB in Building A, Room A429. This event brought together librarians from various universities in Bekasi city and district to discuss the latest developments and strategies for preparing university libraries for library accreditation. This session discussed 9 assessment components consisting of library collection assessment, facilities and infrastructure, services, library staff, library organization and management, innovation and creativity, reading interest level, and community literacy development index. This event received a good response and active participation from the participants, which was followed by a joint discussion to carry out other activities in the future. (Windi Ayu/Librarian).
