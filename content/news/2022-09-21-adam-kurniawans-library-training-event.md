On Thursday (15/09), Adam Kurniawan Library held an event titled “How to Use Springer Journal” for the President University students in Charles Himawan Auditorium. Mr. Deden Ramdhani from the iGroup came as an expert in the event giving training to the students on how to use Springer. The event was conducted in two sessions. The first session was from 09.00 until 10.00 AM and the second one was from 02.00 until 03.00 PM.

The event was meant for the students to practice and have the experience to search for information on the Springer platform. Mr. Ramdhan taught the students how to track and choose articles, journals, and also e-books. After attending the event, the students are expected to get information about various subjects in the e-journals collection and how to search for them on the platform. (Anindya/Librarian)
