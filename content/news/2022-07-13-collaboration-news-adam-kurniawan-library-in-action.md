On Tuesday (21/06), as a follow-up to the mutual agreement between the Bekasi Regency Government and President University No 018/PU/DN/IV/2022 regarding the Development of the Academic Health System, The Head of Adam Kurniawan Library, Neilany Edwina Nulampau, S.Sos., M.Si. and her staffs, David Maraharja Manullang, S. Hum. and Anindya Ambar Wati, S.S.I., M.Hum. visited the Bekasi Regional Archives and Library Office.

The visit was to establish further collaboration between the President University and Bekasi Regency Government; such as conducting seminars, library development, and increasing community literacy.

The head of the Adam Kurniawan Library donated several books to Mrs. Hj. Nurhamidah, M.Pd. who acted as the Head of the Regional Library. The collaboration aims to support the transformation of the President University university library to be more social inclusive. (Anindya/Librarian)
