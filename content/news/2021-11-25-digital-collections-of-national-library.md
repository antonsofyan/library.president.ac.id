In accordance with the circular letter of the head of the National Library of Indonesia, Adam Kurniawan’s library would like to inform you that as the academic community members of the President’s University, we are granted access by the National Library to explore the digital collections, namely:

1.  **iPusnas**, consists of full-text domestically published e-books that can be accessed for free by users by downloading applications from the app store, play store or [http://ipusnas.id](http://ipusnas.id)
2.  **e-Resources National Library** contain e-books, e-journals, and audiobooks published in Indonesia and abroad which can be accessed by users through the website [https://e-resources-perpusnas.go.id](https://e-resources-perpusnas.go.id). To be able to access the collection, the user must register as a member of the National Library through the link [https://keanggotaan.perpusnas.go.id](https://keanggotaan.perpusnas.go.id)

Hopefully this information is useful to improve digital literacy in the President University environment.
