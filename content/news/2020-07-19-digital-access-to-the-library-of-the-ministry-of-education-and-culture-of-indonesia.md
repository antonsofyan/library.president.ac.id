Dear students and lecturers,

To enrich your access to various reading material during COVID-19 pandemic, the Ministry of Education of Culture of Indonesia (Kemendikbud) has made its library service online to let the public gain access to its digital collection, as follows:

1.  **Kemendikbud Institutional Repository**  
    Digital information resource and open-access for publications by Kemendikbud.  
    Application: EPrints – [http://repositori.kemdikbud.go.id/](http://repositori.kemdikbud.go.id/)
2.  **Kemendikbud Online Journal**  
    Online journals published by Kemendikbud (Primary Unit and UPT).  
    Application: OJS – [https://perpustakaan.kemdikbud.go.id/jurnal-kemendikbud](https://perpustakaan.kemdikbud.go.id/jurnal-kemendikbud)
3.  **Kemendikbud Digital Library**  
    The digital library collection of Kemendikbud consisting of open-access reading material.  
    Application: SLiMS – [http://pustaka-digital.kemdikbud.go.id/slims/](http://pustaka-digital.kemdikbud.go.id/slims/)
4.  **Eperpusdikbud (Android version)**  
    The Android version of the digital library mobile application consisting of commercial digital collections that can be accessed by the public.  
    Application: Android App – [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.appsfoundry.eperpuswli.id.kemdikbud.ri&hl=in](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.appsfoundry.eperpuswli.id.kemdikbud.ri&hl=in)
