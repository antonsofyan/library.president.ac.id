On September 25, 2024, the engaging webinar “Emerald: Innovative Approaches to Academic Learning” featured keynote speaker Deden Ramdhani, S.Sos, an Account Executive and Product Specialist from iGroup. This online event attracted a diverse audience, including students and educators keen to explore new methodologies in academic learning.

Deden Ramdhani’s presentation focused on the transformative potential of innovative learning strategies and technologies. He discussed various approaches that can enhance the educational experience, highlighting the role of platforms like Emerald in providing valuable resources and tools for both students and educators.

The session included practical examples of how these innovative methods can be integrated into academic curricula, fostering engagement and improving learning outcomes. Participants actively contributed to the discussion, posing questions and sharing their own experiences with different learning techniques.

Overall, the webinar served as an insightful exploration of modern educational practices, equipping attendees with fresh ideas to enhance their academic pursuits. The event fostered a collaborative atmosphere, encouraging continuous dialogue about innovation in education. (Windi Ayu Setianingrum/Librarian)
