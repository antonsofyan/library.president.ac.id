SMA Terang Bangsa Cirebon paid a visit to the Adam Kurniawan library on Thursday, November 10, 2022. The visit was meant for the students to know the types of library services and facilities. They also got information about literacy activities that are being carried out in Adam Kurniawan Library. (Anindya/Librarian, Aris Masruri/Editor)
