Thursday (18/01) Adam Kurniawan Library (AKL) held an information literacy event with the theme “Confident or Not: How Students Explain Their Confidence on Campus”. An excellent source of advice on courage and how to accomplish goals with amazing confidence was Adam Kurniawan, Library Ambassador. Students from different batch of President University attended the event, event was held at Bank Indonesia Corner.

The speakers at this occasion included Fikram Maulana Aziz, Adinda Isya Handy Putri, and Shafa Aulia Sabrina. They respond to the inquiries they have received and offer advice on boosting self-assurance. They say that aside from having confidence in oneself, the keys to success are having a supportive environment, managing your time well, and having a strong desire to realize your dreams. It is believed that by attending this event, attendees would get the self-assurance necessary to support both academic and extracurricular activities. (Anindya/Librarian).
