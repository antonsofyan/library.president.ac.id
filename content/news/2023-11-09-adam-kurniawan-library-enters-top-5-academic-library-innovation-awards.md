(Tuesday, 10/11) The 2023 Academic Library Innovation Award (ALIA) and Indonesian Academic Librarian Award (IALA) Competition was held by the Indonesian Higher Education Library Forum (FPPTI) West Java Region at the UPI Library.

The main theme is “Upscaling Academic Library Resources as a Strategy to Navigate the Post-Pandemic Era, Digital Transformation, and Society 5.0 in the Interconnected World, IALA & ALIA 2023”.

The UPI, UNIKOM, Telkom University, Bandung Institute of Technology, and Adam Kurniawan Library President University are the libraries that have qualified for the 2023 ALIA Grand Final.

Herli Bahtiar Saleh, M.I.Kom., from the UPI Library, Lusi Satia Rahmawati, S.Sos., from the Telkom University Library, Sri Mulyani, S.I.Pus., from the UNIKOM Library, and Wawan Darmawan, M.I.Kom., from the Maranatha Christian University are contestants who qualify for the Grand Finalist category in the IALA category

The winners of the IALA and ALIA 2023 FPPTI West Java competitions will be sent to take part in the IALA and ALIA competitions from the Central FPPTI to compete with champions from other FPPTI Regions on 1-3 November 2023 in Semarang.
