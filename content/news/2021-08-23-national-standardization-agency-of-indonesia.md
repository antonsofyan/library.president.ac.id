##### **Provided access to documents of standardization national of Indonesia (SNI) for the public through services:**

1.  Free access of SNI full text through  
    [https://akses-sni.bsn.go.id/](https://akses-sni.bsn.go.id/)  
    \*except for identical adoption SNI of foreign standard/international in term of publication copyright
2.  Free download the newest SNI through SISPK  
    [http://sispk.bsn.go.id/](http://sispk.bsn.go.id/)  
    \*valid for one year the SNI is validated, except for identical adoption SNI of foreign standard/international
3.  For document standard purchase through Pesta Online  
    [https://pesta.bsn.go.id/](https://pesta.bsn.go.id/)  
    for fee based on PP No. 40/2018 about PNBP BSN

The newest publication can be accessed through **LibriStania-BSN Library**
