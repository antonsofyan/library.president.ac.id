On Tuesday (05/07 and 12/07), Adam Kurniawan Library held an event titled Literacy Information for the President University students in B. I Corner. The event was used to inform the students regarding the services and facilities that can be provided to support the learning process and the completion of studies. The session was led by Mrs. Neilany Edwina Nulampau, S.Sos., M.Si., The Head of the Adam Kurniawan Library, as the Keynote Speaker along with Mr. Alfonsus Oki Tindarana, S.Hum., Mrs. Anindya Ambarwati, S.S.I., M.Hum., Mrs. Meylda Bakara S.I.Pus., and Mr. David Maraharja Manullang, S. Hum., library staffs who were also involved in the session.

From the event, the students are expected to get information about various library services they need like circulation services, reference services, Turnitin service, Reserve Collection, E-Journals collection, magazines collection, Discussion Rooms, Study Rooms, B. I Corner, etc. To close the event, a library tour was held (Anindya/Librarian).
