The Adam Kurniawan Library (AKL) presented an information literacy session on Wednesday, March 6th 2024 with Salsabila Rizqina as the keynote speaker. Salsabila Rizqina, as a student in the management study program batch 2022, was chosen to share some advice about campus life hack. Salsabila is an exceptional student with lots of experience and achievements from both campus-wide and national events.

The key of the points she made were time management techniques and advice on how to develop strong relationships with Lecturer. Salsabila also emphasized to the attendees the importance of having a vision that can help them achieve both academic and non-academic goals. With this event, it is intended that students would inspire one another to keep striving for success. (Anindya/Librarian).
