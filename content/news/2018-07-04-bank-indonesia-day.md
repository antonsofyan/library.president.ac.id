Do you know that every July 5, since 1946 Indonesia commemorate Bank Indonesia Day?

![ ](https://library.president.ac.id/assets/img/news/pages-13/isi-konten-03.jpg)

On this date, was born the first state-owned bank after the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, namely PT. Bank Negara Indonesia (Persero) Tbk or BNI. Born in the struggle for independence of the Republic of Indonesia, BNI was used as a central bank and a commercial bank set forth in Government Regulation in Lieu of Law No. 2 of 1946, before finally working as a commercial bank since 1955. The Republic of Indonesia or ORI as the first official issued by the Government of Indonesia on October 30, 1946 and circulated by Bank Negara Indonesia.

Following the appointment of De Javache Bank which is the legacy of the Dutch Government as the central bank in 1949, the Government as the central bank BNI. BNI was defined as a development bank and granted the right to act as a foreign exchange bank in 1950 with direct access to foreign transactions.
