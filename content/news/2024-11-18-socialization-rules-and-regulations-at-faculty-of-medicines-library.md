On November 14th, 2024 A library rules and regulations socializing event was held at the Khayangan Campus of the Faculty of Medicine to acquaint students with the library policies. Key policies on library behavior, borrowing practices, and using digital resources like e-books and academic databases were reviewed in the event that medical students attended. Neilany Edwina Nulampau, S.Sos., M.Si. as the library head, emphasized how crucial it is to abide by these rules to preserve an effective and welcoming learning environment. A Q&A session was also included in the program to encourage both individual and group learning. (Windi Ayu/Librarian)
