On Tuesday (06/06), as a follow-up to the joint agreement between the Bekasi Regency Government and President University No. 018/PU/DN/IV/2022 concerning the Development of an Academic Health System. Adam Kurniawan Library collaborated with the Bekasi Regency Government Archives and Library Office to improve the culture of literacy in the community by establishing the librarian profession as an information agent.

Located at the Department of Archives and Libraries of the Bekasi Regency Government, the Head of Adam Kurniawan Library, Neilany Edwina Nulampau, S.Sos, M.Si as a speaker shared information about experiences in the field of library and information science, the responsibilities of being the Head of Adam Kurniawan Library, and strategies to improve the culture of literacy in the community through library programs. (Anindya/Librarian).
