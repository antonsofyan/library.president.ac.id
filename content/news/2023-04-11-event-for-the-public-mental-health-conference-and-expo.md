On Thursday (06/04), Adam Kurniawan Library held an event in collaboration with LAW students class of 2022 President University entitled “Mental Health Conference and Expo”. The keynote speakers were Ms. Zenny Rezania Dewantary the founder of SalingJaga.id, Ms. Dinda Chairsha as the Chair of the Indonesian Mental Health Ambassador, and Ms. Josephine Savitri Dinarbrata, S.Psi as Assistant Psychologist. This live and interactive conference provided ample opportunity to ask questions and share successes, challenges, and best practices to prevent bullying.

After the 30-minute webinar, participants were informed about further opportunities and support from the anti-bullying team.

The conference and exhibition are for all school staff, educators, or youth facilitators who would like guidance on various topics about anti-bullying work in schools and universities. (Anindya/Librarian).
